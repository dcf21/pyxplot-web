4.1.2 Upper and Lower Limit Data Points

Pyxplot can plot data points using the standard upper- and lower-limit symbols. No special syntax is required for this; these symbols are pointtypes†The pointtype modifier was introduced in Section 2.9. 12 and 13 respectively, obtained as follows: 

plot 'upperlimits.dat' with points pointtype 12
plot 'lowerlimits.dat' with points pointtype 13
