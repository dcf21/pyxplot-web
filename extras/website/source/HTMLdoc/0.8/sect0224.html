B.3 The Multiplot Environment

Gnuplot’s multiplot environment, used for placing many graphs alongside one another, is massively extended in PyXPlot. As well as making it much easier to produce galleries of plots and inset graphs, a wide range of vector graphs objects can also be added to the multiplot canvas. This is described in detail in Chapter 3.
