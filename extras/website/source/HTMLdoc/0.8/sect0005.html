1.4 Adding Other Vector Graphics Objects

In addition to graphs, a range of other objects can be placed on multiplot canvases:

  • Arcs of circles (the arc command).

  • Arrows (the arrow command).

  • Rectangular boxes (the box command).

  • Circles (the circle command).

  • Ellipses (the ellipse command).

  • Encapsulated PostScript images (the eps command).

  • Graphical images in bmp, gif, jpeg or png formats (the image command).

  • Lines (the line command).

  • Piecharts (the piechart command).

  • Points labelled by crosses and other symbols (the point command).

  • Text labels (the text command).

Put together, these commands can be used to produce a wide range of vector graphics. In the remainder of this chapter, we describe these commands in turn, providing a variety of examples of their use.
